Who Made All This?

A Dhikr Lift-the-Flap Book

Ages 3+ Interactive Flap & Mirror Book Quran & Sunnah Based

Who Made All This? is a gentle, interactive Islamic children's book that helps young hearts remember Allah, reflect on His creation, and thank Him through simple daily dhikr.

Through the joyful journey of a curious little ladybug, children explore the world around them, the sky, the earth, and the many blessings Allah has created. Each lift-the-flap moment encourages children to pause, observe, and remember that Allah is the Creator of everything.

This book introduces essential dhikr phrases in a playful and meaningful way, helping children understand both the words and their meanings:

  • SubḥānAllāh — Glory be to Allah
  • Alḥamdulillāh — All praise is for Allah
  • Allāhu Akbar — Allah is the Greatest
  • Mā shā' Allāh — What Allah has willed
  • TabārakAllāh — Blessed is Allah

Each dhikr is paired with child-friendly explanations, reflections on Allah's creation, and authentic references from the Qur'an, nurturing early tadabbur and gratitude.

At the end of the book, children are invited to look into a mirror, gently reminding them that Allah not only made the world around them... He made them too!

With engaging illustrations, lift-the-flap elements, and simple activities, this book supports early Islamic learning, vocabulary development, and joyful remembrance of Allah.

This book is perfect for:

  • Toddlers and preschoolers (Ages 3+)
  • Islamic homeschooling and bed time reading
